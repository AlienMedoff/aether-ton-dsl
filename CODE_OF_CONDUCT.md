# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who participates in this project, regardless of:
- Experience level
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Personal appearance
- Body size
- Race
- Ethnicity
- Age
- Religion
- Nationality

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- **Be respectful**: Treat others with dignity and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome newcomers and diverse perspectives
- **Be collaborative**: Work together and share knowledge
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be patient**: Remember that everyone has different experience levels
- **Be accountable**: Take responsibility for your actions and mistakes

### Unacceptable Behavior
- **Harassment**: Unwelcome comments, advances, or jokes
- **Discrimination**: Negative behavior based on identity or background
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, name-calling, or ad hominem attacks
- **Spam**: Unrelated content, excessive self-promotion
- **Disruption**: Interfering with others' ability to participate
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing private information without consent
- **Threats**: Any form of intimidation or threat

## Reporting

### How to Report
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Immediate Response**: 
   - Contact moderators directly
   - Use platform reporting tools
   - Remove yourself from the situation if needed

2. **Formal Report**:
   - Email: conduct@aether-os.com
   - GitHub: Report issue with "conduct" label
   - Include: What happened, when, where, who was involved

3. **Confidentiality**: 
   - Reports will be kept confidential
   - Only involved parties will be informed
   - Anonymous reporting available upon request

### Response Process
- **Acknowledgment**: Within 24 hours
- **Investigation**: Within 48 hours
- **Resolution**: Within 1 week for most cases
- **Follow-up**: Check in with reporter if needed

## Enforcement

### Consequences
Depending on severity and frequency:

#### First Offense
- Private warning from moderators
- Request to edit/remove inappropriate content
- Educational resources provided

#### Second Offense
- Temporary suspension (1-7 days)
- Required review of Code of Conduct
- Public acknowledgment of rules

#### Severe or Repeated Offenses
- Permanent ban from project spaces
- Removal from contributor lists
- Reporting to platform administrators if needed

### Appeals
- Appeals can be sent to conduct@aether-os.com
- Reviewed by different moderator team members
- Decision within 3 business days

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

### Project Spaces
- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and wikis
- Official Discord/Telegram channels
- Community events and meetups

### Project Activities
- Code contributions and reviews
- Documentation and translations
- Community management and moderation
- Conference presentations and talks

### Online Behavior
- Social media interactions about the project
- Blog posts and articles
- Private messages related to project work

## Additional Guidelines

### For Maintainers
- Lead by example in positive behavior
- Respond promptly to conduct reports
- Apply consequences consistently and fairly
- Provide clear explanations for decisions
- Protect privacy of all involved parties

### For Community Members
- Help newcomers feel welcome
- Assume good faith in interactions
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Report concerns promptly and privately
- Participate in learning opportunities

### For Event Organizers
- Ensure safe and inclusive environments
- Have clear conduct policies for events
- Provide multiple ways to report issues
- Train staff on conduct procedures
- Consider accessibility needs
